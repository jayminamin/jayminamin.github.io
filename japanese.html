Kanji (漢字)

Ideograms from China. They represent meanings. Example: 私 (watashi - I).

Hiragana (ひらがな)

The primary phonetic script for native words and grammatical particles.

Katakana (カタカナ)

Used for loan words (phonetically borrowed) like テレビ (TV) or カメラ (Camera).
